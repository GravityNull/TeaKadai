Flash News on the go

GravityNull TeaKadai is a simple flash news web app, developed and maintained by Rooban
Using this app you can find the all over Tamil flash news happening in and around Tamilnadu

Powered by Google's CSE & X aka Twitter's LIST API

GravityNull fetches the flash news from the Google's trusted Programmable Search Engine and official thirdpary's Twitter aka X tweets, with the help of a curated List API.
